Our Mission

KAVACH was born from a simple observation: security teams are drowning in data but starving for intelligence. Traditional SIEM solutions generate endless alerts, but they lack the cognitive ability to understand how these alerts connect into coordinated attacks.

We set out to solve this fundamental problem by building an AI-powered platform that doesn't just collect data—it understands it. KAVACH automatically reconstructs complete cyber kill chains, revealing the full scope of attacks from initial reconnaissance to data exfiltration. This empowers security teams to respond faster, investigate deeper, and defend more effectively.

Our mission is to democratize advanced threat intelligence, making enterprise-grade cyber defense accessible to organizations of all sizes. We believe that every organization deserves the ability to understand and respond to sophisticated cyber attacks.

Why KAVACH?

The name "KAVACH" comes from Sanskrit, meaning "shield" or "armor." In Indian mythology, KAVACH represents divine protection—an impenetrable defense against threats. This perfectly embodies our mission: to be the shield that protects organizations from sophisticated cyber attacks.

Like ancient armor that protected warriors in battle, our platform provides continuous, autonomous protection for modern digital infrastructure. We believe this protection should be intelligent, adaptive, and always vigilant—qualities that define KAVACH.
